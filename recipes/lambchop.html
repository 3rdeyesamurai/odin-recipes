Lamb chops recipe

Grilled Lamb chops

Ingredients

  • Lambchops
  • Thyme
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika
  • garlic
  1. Marinate Lambchops in seasonings
  2. Start fire on the grill using charcoal
  3. let fire die down
  4. place lamb chops on grill
  5. grill on each side for 10 minutes
