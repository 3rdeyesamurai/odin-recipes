Afredo pasta sauce

A rich and creamy parmesean and cream sauce good for pasta or ravioli!

Ingredients

  • 1 quart heavy cream
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 2 cups Parmesean cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. Melt butter in a pan
  2. Add heavy cream
  3. Bring heavy cream to a low boil
  4. Add Parmesean cheese
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
